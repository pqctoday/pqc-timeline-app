# Executive Order 14306 - Strengthening the Nation's Cybersecurity (PQC Provisions)

## Authors

**Organization:** White House

## Scope

**Industries:** Federal Government; All Sectors
**Region:** USA
**Document type:** Compliance/Policy

## How It Relates to PQC

Federal PQC procurement mandate

**Dependencies:** FIPS 203/204/205; CNSA 2.0

## PQC Risk Profile

**Harvest Now, Decrypt Later:** **HIGH** — Encrypted data captured today can be decrypted by a future quantum computer (harvest-now-decrypt-later attack). Adopting this specification is critical to protect long-lived confidential data.

**Identity & Authentication Integrity:** Not directly addressed by this document.

**Digital Signature Integrity:** Not directly addressed by this document.

**Migration urgency:** High

## Short Description

Trump administration EO directing DHS/CISA to publish PQC product categories. Requires TLS 1.3 adoption by 2030. Amends EO 13694 and EO 14144. Retains PQC migration provisions from Biden era.
