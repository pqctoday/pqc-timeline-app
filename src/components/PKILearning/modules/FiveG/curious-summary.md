Curious about what happens under the hood of your 5G connection? 

**The Problem:** In older mobile networks, your phone's unique identity was broadcasted openly, allowing attackers to track you. 5G completely hides this identity through encryption, but future quantum computers could eventually break that encryption.
**The Solution:** Telecommunication systems must upgrade to advanced "Post-Quantum Cryptography" that even future supercomputers cannot crack.
**The Strategy:** Replace the current mathematical locks defending mobile privacy and authentication with quantum-proof protocols before quantum computers become a reality.
**The Ecosystem:** This upgrade impacts mobile phone manufacturers, telecom networks, and global network standards bodies.
