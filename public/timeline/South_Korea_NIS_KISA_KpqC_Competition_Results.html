Selected Algorithms from the KpqC Competition Round 2

(January 16, 2025)

After careful consideration during Round 2 of the KpqC competition, the KpqC team is pleased to announce the final algorithms.

Round2 candidates
Digital Signature PKE/KEM
AIMer NTRU+
HAETAE SMAUG-T

As the KpqC competition comes to an end, we would like to express our sincere gratitude to all the teams that participated in Round 1 and Round 2 for their dedication and hard work. We would also like to thank the public reviewers for their valuable feedback and interest in the KpqC algorithms.

Thanks to the efforts and participation of all involved, we have been able to achieve our primary objectives: advancing research in Korea's PQC field, fostering both domestic and international technology exchange, and contributing to the development of human resources in Korea's PQC domain.

Finally, we would like to extend our deepest thanks to the organizations, anonymous supporters, and individuals whose contributions helped make this competition a success.
