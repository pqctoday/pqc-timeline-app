# Compliance & Regulatory Strategy

## Overview

The Compliance & Regulatory Strategy module teaches executives how to navigate the complex and evolving landscape of PQC compliance requirements across jurisdictions. It covers jurisdiction mapping to identify applicable frameworks and deadline conflicts, audit readiness checklisting with 25 evidence items across 5 categories, and compliance timeline building that overlays organizational milestones on regulatory deadlines with gap analysis. The module integrates live data from the app's compliance framework database and country timeline system.

## Key Concepts

- **Major PQC Compliance Frameworks** — CNSA 2.0 (NSA, US national security systems), NIST IR 8547 (US federal guidance), ETSI (European standards), ANSSI (French national agency), BSI (German federal office)
- **Compliance-First vs. Risk-First Strategy** — two approaches: compliance-first (driven by regulatory deadlines, ensures certification) vs. risk-first (driven by threat assessment, prioritizes highest-risk systems)
- **Jurisdiction Mapping** — multi-select jurisdiction picker showing which compliance frameworks apply, earliest deadlines, and cross-jurisdiction conflicts (e.g., CNSA 2.0 vs. ETSI algorithm preferences, hybrid vs. pure PQC requirements)
- **Conflict Detection** — identifies deadline divergence (>3 years apart), algorithm preference conflicts between frameworks, and hybrid vs. pure PQC requirement mismatches
- **Audit Readiness** — 25-item checklist across 5 categories: cryptographic inventory (5), policy & governance (5), technical controls (5), vendor management (5), evidence & documentation (5)
- **Compliance Timeline** — framework deadlines and country milestones overlaid with organizational milestones; gap analysis showing which deadlines are completed, on-track, or at-risk
- **Key Compliance Deadlines** — NSA CNSA 2.0 software (2025-2030), NIST deprecation of classical algorithms (2030-2035), ANSSI hybrid requirements (2025-2030)

## Workshop / Interactive Activities

The workshop has 3 interactive steps:

1. **Jurisdiction Mapper** — select from 10 jurisdictions across 3 regions; see matching compliance frameworks, earliest deadlines, and automatically detected conflicts; export as Markdown/CSV
2. **Audit Readiness Checklist** — 5-section, 25-item checklist with pre-populated items when assessment is complete; generates readiness percentage and structured evidence checklist; saves as executive document
3. **Compliance Timeline Builder** — interactive timeline with external deadlines from country and framework data; gap analysis computing completion status for each deadline; visual indicators for at-risk deadlines
